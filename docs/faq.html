Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Visit treasurepavilion.net and click the "Sign Up" button. Complete the registration form with your email and create a secure password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

What information do I need to provide?

Basic account creation requires an email address and password. For enhanced features and higher transaction limits, you may need to complete identity verification (KYC).

Is there a minimum deposit?

No minimum deposit is required to create an account. However, to begin trading, you'll need to fund your account with your preferred digital asset.

How do I deposit funds?

After logging in, navigate to the "Deposit" section. Select your preferred digital asset and follow the instructions to transfer funds to your TrePav wallet.

How to Participate

How do I start trading?

Once your account is funded, navigate to the trading interface. Select the assets you want to trade, enter your desired quantity, and execute your trade. Rewards are calculated and distributed automatically.

What are the trading fees?

TrePav charges minimal fees. Instead of taking commissions, we reward your participation through our reward mechanism. Check the platform for current fee structure.

How do I earn rewards?

Rewards are earned automatically on every transaction based on your membership tier. The more you trade, the higher your tier, and the better your reward rate (1.8% - 4.1%).

What is the referral program?

Share your unique referral link with friends. When they join and trade, you earn commissions on their activity. The more active your referrals, the more you earn.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Navigate to the "Withdraw" section in your account. Select your preferred digital asset and withdrawal address. Withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours.

Common Questions

Is TrePav safe?

Yes. TrePav employs enterprise-grade security measures including multi-signature wallets, encryption, and regular security audits. Your assets are protected by industry-leading protocols.

What assets can I trade?

TrePav supports a wide range of digital assets including cryptocurrencies, digital collectibles, and other blockchain-based assets. Check the platform for the complete list of supported assets.

How are rewards calculated?

Rewards are calculated based on your transaction volume, membership tier, and the specific asset being traded. Calculations are transparent and displayed in real-time on your dashboard.

Can I lose money on TrePav?

Like all trading platforms, the value of digital assets can fluctuate. While TrePav provides rewards and stable pricing mechanisms, you should only trade with funds you can afford to lose.

What is the membership tier system?

TrePav has 6 membership tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Elite) based on your trading volume. Higher tiers unlock better reward rates and exclusive features.

How do I upgrade my tier?

Tiers are based on cumulative trading volume. As you trade more, you automatically progress to higher tiers and unlock better rewards.

Is there customer support?

Yes. TrePav provides 24/7 customer support. Contact us at contact@treasurepavilion.net or visit treasurepavilion.net for support options.

What if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password securely.

Can I use TrePav on mobile?

Yes. TrePav is accessible on mobile devices through our responsive web platform. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon.
